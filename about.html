Who Am I ?

Hi, I'm Anıl. I was born on June 10, 2004, in İzmir. After completing my high school education at 85th Year Anatolian High School, I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Programming at Uşak University due to my keen interest in the future of technology. I chose to become a programmer as I believe the future lies in technology. Growing up alongside computers since the age of 4, I formatted my computer at the age of 12 and began making hardware changes at the age of 13.

During my studies, I learned various programming languages and started to develop myself in areas where I felt proficient. In my free time, I researched and learned languages related to my interests, striving to enhance my skills. I worked on various projects to improve myself.

Currently, I work as a Junior Developer, and I am dedicated to continuous self-improvement every day. Conducting research on technology and working on new projects motivates me. I aim to be a successful and well-equipped developer in the future.
